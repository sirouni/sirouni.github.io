3D Block Puzzle

How to Play

Drag and drop 3D blocks onto the board. Clear complete rows, columns, or entire planes to score points. The game ends when no more pieces can be placed.

Game Modes

  • Casual 5×5 — A compact 3-layer board. Perfect for quick sessions.
  • Classic 8×8 — A larger board with deeper strategic challenges.

Controls

  • Place blocks — Drag a piece from the tray onto the board.
  • Rotate camera — Swipe on the board area to rotate your view.
  • Layer view — Tap the layer button to explode layers apart.
  • Undo — Tap the undo button to reverse your last move.
