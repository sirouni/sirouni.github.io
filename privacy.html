Privacy Policy

Trilayer: 3D Block Puzzle

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

Trilayer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Data We Collect

Trilayer uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how players interact with the game so we can improve it. The data collected includes:

• App launch and session duration
• Game mode played and scores achieved
• Feature usage (e.g., camera rotation, sharing)
• Device type and OS version (anonymized)
• App language setting

TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not track users across apps, and does not collect IP addresses or device identifiers. All data is aggregated and anonymous. Learn more at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

• No names, email addresses, or contact information
• No location data
• No advertising identifiers
• No cross-app tracking
• No data sold to third parties

Game Center

Trilayer supports Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not access your Game Center profile data beyond your public leaderboard score.

Children's Privacy

Trilayer does not knowingly collect any data from children. The anonymous analytics described above apply equally to all users regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
